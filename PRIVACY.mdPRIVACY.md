# Privacy Policy - ClipRythm

Last updated: April 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
ClipRythm collects only the OAuth tokens necessary to post content to your connected social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram). No personal data is collected or stored beyond what is required for authentication.

## 2. How We Use Information
Tokens are used solely to publish short-form videos to your connected accounts on your behalf. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

## 3. Data Storage
Authentication tokens are stored locally on your own device. ClipRythm does not operate any external servers that store your personal information.

## 4. Third-Party Services
ClipRythm integrates with TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.
